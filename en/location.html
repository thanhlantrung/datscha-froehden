how to find us

Fröhdener Mühlenstr. 2, Jüterbog OT Fröhden.

We are located in Teltow Fläming, which is known as a 'skater paradise'. With over 230km of flat paths through villages, forests and fields, it is perfect for bike riding and rollerblading.

By car, we are one hour away from Neukölln.

By train: the train (RE4 and RE5) runs roughly every hour from Berlin Südkreuz to Jüterbog and takes 40 minutes. From Jüterbog station, our house is an easy 10km bike ride.

Fröhden is a village of roughly 240 people, neighbouring Markendorf, which has around the same number of inhabitants. There is a cafe in our village, that serves breakfast and homemade cakes.
