how to find us

We are located at Fröhdener Mühlenstr. 2, Jüterbog OT Fröhden.

email: hello[at]datschafroehden.de

From Berlin, by train: the train (RE4 and RE5) run roughly every hour from Südkreuz to Jüterbog and takes 40 minutes. From Jüterbog station, you can either ride your bike 10km (takes roughly 30-40 mins) to Fröhden. Alternatively, you can catch the bus here (with a change in Jüterbog Stadt).

We definitely recommend taking your bike here, as Fröhden is part of Teltow Fläming, which is over 230km of rollerblade and bike path, through fields, forests and villages.
