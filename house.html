the story

After traveling for a year throughout Asia, we decided our newest adventure would be to breathe new life into an abandoned house in Brandenburg. We grow our own vegetables and keep chickens not only for their eggs, but their company, and aim to live a slow and more sustainable life. We enjoy sharing our space with others. We want Datscha Fröhden to reflect what we believe in - an escape from the capitalist rat race of the outside world.

the space

Our house was built in 1920. We have 1700m2 land, a large part in which we dedicate to our vegetable garden.

We can accommodate 11 people in beds/sofa beds, plus additional people in sleeping bags/tents.

We also have a coworking space.
