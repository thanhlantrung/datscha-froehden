the space

Our house was built in the 1920s. We have 1700m2 of land, a large part in which we dedicate to our vegetable garden.

We can accommodate 11 people in beds/sofa beds, plus additional people in sleeping bags/tents.

We also have 40m2 room which we use as a coworking space.
